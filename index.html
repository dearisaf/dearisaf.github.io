About Me

With over a decade of experience in the fashion industry, I specialize in blending traditional Indonesian textiles with modern, sustainable design. My work bridges the gap between contemporary fashion and Indonesia’s cultural heritage, using batik, ikat, and tenun to create stylish, eco-friendly pieces.

A graduate of Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) Surabaya, I founded my label, "Karya Nusantara", in Surabaya, focusing on elevating traditional craftsmanship. I’ve showcased my collections at Surabaya Fashion Parade and Indonesia Fashion Week in Jakarta, gaining recognition for modernizing Indonesian textiles.

I’ve collaborated with notable brands like Batik Keris and Matahari Department Store, contributing to their sustainable initiatives. My designs have dressed influencers and celebrities at Jakarta Fashion Week and other high-profile events.

Featured in Elle Indonesia and Harper's Bazaar Indonesia, I’m dedicated to building a brand that celebrates Indonesia's fashion legacy while addressing global sustainability challenges.
